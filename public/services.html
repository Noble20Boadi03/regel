Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How far in advance should I book my appointment?

We recommend booking at least 1-2 weeks in advance for standard services and 3-4 weeks for special occasions like weddings or major events. However, we do our best to accommodate last-minute appointments when possible.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Appointments cancelled with less than 24 hours notice may be subject to a cancellation fee of 50% of the service cost.

Do you use cruelty-free products?

Yes, we are committed to using cruelty-free and vegan products whenever possible. Many of our brands are Leaping Bunny certified, and we're happy to discuss product options with you.

How long will my service results last?

Service longevity varies: gel manicures last 2-3 weeks, acrylic nails 3-4 weeks with fills, lash extensions 2-3 weeks with proper care, and makeup varies by type but typically lasts through your event with minimal touch-ups.
